Risk Score is a metric that helps to estimate the chance that an address is related to illegal activity. The value can range from 0% to 100%, where 0% means that the address is safe, and 100% means that the address is involved in illegal activity. If the address belongs to an entity, its risk score is the same as the entity’s risk score.
